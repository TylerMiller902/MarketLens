Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026  ·  Last updated: May 2026

The short version: We collect your Google account info (name, email, profile picture) when you sign in, and we store your portfolio data in our database. We don't sell your data. Ever.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive from Google:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile picture URL
  • A unique Google account identifier

We do not receive or store your Google password.

Portfolio Data

If you add positions to your portfolio, we store:

  • Stock ticker symbols and company names
  • Number of shares held
  • Cost basis per share
  • Date positions were added

Usage Data

We do not currently collect analytics, page views, or behavioral tracking data. We may log server errors for debugging purposes.

Cookies and Sessions

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains a session identifier (not your personal data) and expires after 30 days. You can delete it at any time by signing out or clearing your browser cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your logged-in session
  • Display your name and profile picture within the app
  • Store and sync your portfolio across devices
  • Improve the stability and performance of the service

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway, a cloud platform based in the United States. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with encryption at rest.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

StockScope uses the following third-party services to provide market data:

  • Financial Modeling Prep (FMP) — stock quotes, financials, and company data. Privacy Policy
  • Yahoo Finance — intraday price data and news. Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — authentication. Privacy Policy

We do not share your personal information with these services. They provide market data that we display in the app.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and portfolio data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

6. Children's Privacy

StockScope is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at:

privacy@stockscope.app
