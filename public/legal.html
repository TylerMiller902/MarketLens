Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 31, 2026

The short version: We collect your Google account info when you sign in and store your portfolio data securely. We use Stripe to process payments. We do not sell your personal data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

StockScope is operated by StockScope LLC, a Minnesota limited liability company. Our contact email is thestockscopellc@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile picture URL
  • A unique Google account identifier

We do not receive or store your Google password.

Portfolio Data

If you add positions to your portfolio, we store:

  • Stock ticker symbols and company names
  • Number of shares held
  • Cost basis per share
  • Date positions were added

Payment Information

If you subscribe to StockScope Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. We receive and store a Stripe customer ID and subscription ID to manage your account status. By subscribing, you also agree to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Usage & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (page views, session duration, device type, geography). This data is aggregated and anonymous. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Cookies and Sessions

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains a session identifier (not your personal data) and expires after 30 days. You can delete it by signing out or clearing your browser cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your logged-in session
  • Display your name within the app
  • Store and sync your portfolio across devices
  • Process and manage your Pro subscription via Stripe
  • Send transactional emails related to your subscription (via Stripe)
  • Improve the stability and performance of the service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway in the United States. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with industry-standard security measures.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

StockScope uses the following third-party services:

  • Financial Modeling Prep (FMP) — market data, financials, and company information provided under a commercial license. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions. Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — account authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics. Privacy Policy
  • Railway — cloud hosting and database. Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and portfolio data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing thestockscopellc@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request. Note that Stripe may retain payment records as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

StockScope is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at thestockscopellc@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
